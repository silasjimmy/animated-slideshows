About us

Cyeco Limited, is a bicycle leasing company that started out as a passion project by the founders who are avid cyclists. With their zeal to preserve the environment through eco-friendly means of transport, they identified a niche in the market for providing carbon-free mode of transport at an affordable price. Together, the founders decided to start a business to fill the gap and like-minded folks to embrace the culture of cycling.

Based in the Kilifi, a town in the coastal region of Kenya, Cyeco Africa is slowly but steadily growing to be the popular provider of eco-friendly means of transport in the country. We offer a variety of bicycle models, including mountain and city bicycles. With Cyeco, you can lease a bicycle for whatever use that fits your needs.

We are currently offering single bookings, group bookings, tours and special bookings for customers with unique travelling arrangements . We are also have offers (coupons) for our top regular customers.

Our goal is to encourage folks to embrace cycling as a mode of transport. By doing so, we aim to reduce the level of carbon emissions and promote a healthy lifestyle.

Pedal your way to anywhere

Cycling as a way to commute presents a plethora of benefits which extend beyond mere transportation. Beyond the financial savings garnered from bypassing fuel costs and parking fees, cycling makes it easier to navigate through urban landscapes effortlessly and evading congested traffic, making it a convinient and flexible mode of transport.

Wheels of freedom

Embarking on cycling adventures allows exploration of landscapes, from rugged terrains to serene countryside trails, providing a unique perspective of the surroundings. The freedom of movement on a bike enables access to off-the-beaten-path locations unreachable by conventional means, fostering a deeper connection with nature and local communities. It cultivates a sense of exploration and discovery, sparking curiosity while uncovering hidden gems along the journey.

Ride, roam, repeat

As a low-impact exercise, cycling is gentle on the joints while providing an incredible full-body workout. Engaging major muscle groups like the legs, glutes, and core, cycling enhances strength, endurance, and muscle tone. Its cardiovascular benefits are remarkable, effectively boosting heart health and improving stamina. Beyond physical benefits, cycling outdoors offers an invigorating experience and fostering mental well-being making it suitable for people of different ages fitness levels.
